Mission

Our mission is to provide comprehensive information about the Thadingyut Festival, also known as the Festival of Lights in Myanmar.This includes details about the festival's historical significance, cultural practices, festivities, and regional traditions. The goal is to educate and engage visitors by showcasing the vibrant celebrations, illuminations, and acts of devotion that take place during this important event.

Vission

Our vission is to celebrate and preserve the cultural heritage of the Thadingyut Festival by providing a comprehensive platform for information, education, and engagement. It aims to highlight the festival's significance, promote cultural understanding, and foster a sense of community among visitors and participants. The web page strives to capture the essence of the Festival of Lights, showcasing its historical roots, traditional practices, and modern-day celebrations.
